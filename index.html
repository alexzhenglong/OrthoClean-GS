Abstract

This work presents Ortho-3DGS, a robust framework for True Digital Orthophoto Map (TDOM) generation from UAV imagery, published in IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (J-STARS). Our method introduces a depth-regulated 3D Gaussian Splatting approach that integrates geometric consistency and appearance modeling for reliable orthophoto reconstruction in weakly dynamic urban environments. Experiments on multiple UAV benchmark datasets demonstrate significant improvements in building edge sharpness, occlusion handling, and suppression of ghosting artifacts compared to state-of-the-art methods and commercial software.
